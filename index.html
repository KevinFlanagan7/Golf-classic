Ballinalsoe Golf Club June 7th-9th

About

Ballinasloe Golf Club is an 18 hole parkland course based in Ireland's Hidden Heartlands. The club was founded in 1894 making it one of Connacht's oldest golf clubs. The competition is open to men and ladies. The format is 18 holes singles stableford played over three days, June 7th-9th 2024.
